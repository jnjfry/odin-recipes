Grilled Cheese

This grilled cheese recipe is simply the best!

We will show you how to make the crispiest and most delicous grilled cheese you've ever had

Ingredients

  • Two slices of sourdough bread (or any sandwhich bread of your choice)
  • 2 tbsp salted Butter
  • 2 slices of your favorite cheese
  • Fresh Ground Pepper
  • Red Pepper Flakes (optional)

Steps

  1. Start preheating your pan on medium heat
  2. Butter one side of each slice of bread
  3. Grind your pepper onto both buttered sides of the bread
  4. Once your pan is hot, but one slice of bread (butter side down) into the pan

    Start constructing your grilled cheese by placing your slices of cheese on the bread, top the cheese with your last slice of bread (butter side up)

    If you are using red pepper flakes, add them inb between your cheese slices

  5. Once your grilled cheese is fully constructed in the pan, add 1 tbsp water and cover the pan with lid until cheese starts to melt (aprox. 5 min)
  6. After the cheese has started to melt, remove lid and flip grilled cheese
  7. Let the grilled cheese to cook for aprox. another 5 min
  8. Serve your grilled cheese and enjoy!
